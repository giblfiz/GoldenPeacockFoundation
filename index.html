About the Golden Peacock Foundation

The Golden Peacock Foundation is a nonprofit organization dedicated to improving access to education and alleviating suffering in underserved communities around the world. We believe that education is can be delightful, and that everyone should have the opportunity to explore their world. We work to empower individuals and communities to overcome the barriers to education and improve their quality of life.

Our Programs

  • The Green Tara Program

    The Green Tara Program is dedicated to providing financial support for Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) for low-income seniors who have lead a life of service. We believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare, and we work to ensure that seniors have the resources they need to maintain their health and well-being. Through the Green Tara Program, we provide grants to cover the cost of TCM herbs, enabling seniors to access these holistic treatments and improve their quality of life.

  • Dinosaur and Natural History Museum

    We are excited to announce our partnership with the GHATS Foundation to build a high traffic dinosaur and natural history museum in Coimbatore, India. This museum will be a unique educational resource for the community, offering interactive exhibits on the natural world and the history of life on Earth. We believe that this museum will be a transformative project for Coimbatore, and we are committed to working with the GOTS Foundation to make it a reality.

    This project is currently in progress, and we will provide updates on our progress and opportunities for support as they become available. We look forward to bringing this exciting museum to life and sharing it with the community.

  • 2022 Thanksgiving Meals for Low-Income Families

    At Thanksgiving, we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to share a warm and nourishing meal with loved ones. That's why we offer a special program to provide Thanksgiving meals for low-income families in our community. Through this program, we provide all the ingredients for a traditional Thanksgiving feast, including a turkey, stuffing, potatoes, vegetables, and pie.
